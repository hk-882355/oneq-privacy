OneQ - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 28, 2026

Introduction

OneQ ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

OneQ stores your daily quest data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

  • Local Data: Your quests, completion status, and streak data are stored only on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • Settings: Your preferences (sound, haptic feedback) are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

OneQ uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data as described in their privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

Data Retention

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

OneQ does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
